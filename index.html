Snipers

At the time of making this website it is Chapter 2 Season 4, So there can be changes to the game by the time you read this but at the moment there are 2 snipers in the game. One being the Suppressed sniper rifle the other being the Bolt Action sniper

Suppresed Sniper

The Suppresed Sniper comes in two rarities: Epic and Legendary. the epic Suppressed sniper does 95 damage to the body with a instant kill 252 damage shot. The legendary Suppressed Sniper does 100 damage to the body and does 262 damage to the head.
